## Description

VisionPlay
See the Game

Train the decision before the ball arrives.

VisionPlay builds the habit elite players rely on: scanning the field, recognizing pressure, and deciding early — so your first action is faster and more effective when you receive.

Short, focused reps. Clear audio cues. Real feedback you can use on the pitch.

Built for how you actually train

• Solo — Train on your own with guided reps
• Partner — iPad as the display, coach phone controls the session
• Timed sessions — Structured blocks for full workouts

No complicated setup. Pick an activity, train, and review your results.

Core activities

• Meet the Ball — Move early and arrive prepared
• Dribble or Pass — Read space or play forward
• Away from Pressure — Find the safe side under pressure
• One Touch Passing — Play fast with your picture made early

Each activity targets a real moment in the game: receiving, carrying, escaping pressure, and playing one-touch.

Progress that matters

Track:
- Accuracy
- Reaction time
- Decision quality

Optional sign-in (Apple or email) syncs your profiles and sessions across devices.

Train without an account — no personal account required to begin training.

Training data may be stored locally and may also be transmitted to support app functionality. When not signed in, data is associated with an anonymous identifier.

Designed for the field

- Bright, readable display for outdoor use
- Audio cues so you don't stare at the screen
- Built for training with the ball, not tapping menus

For players and coaches

Players develop scanning and decision habits rep by rep.

Coaches can run partner sessions from their phone while the player trains on a larger display — ideal for individual training or small groups.

Privacy

Requirements

- iOS 18 or later
- Partner/coach features require a network connection and two devices

## App Review Notes

VisionPlay is a football training app. Core drills use visual and audio cues on screen; no camera or microphone is required for training.

VisionPlay supports multiple player profiles under one authenticated account. Sign in with Apple authenticates the account holder and provides the account holder’s name and email via Authentication Services. The app does **not** ask for name or email again after Sign in with Apple. The first training profile is created automatically from the Apple account name (or a default). Users may optionally add additional athlete profiles later; that is separate from Sign in with Apple identity.

App Privacy: We do not track users. User ID and other declared data types are for App Functionality / Account Management only — not for tracking. No App Tracking Transparency prompt is shown because we do not track.

Solo test path: Open app → start Meet the Ball → complete a short block → view summary.

Optional sign-in: Sign in with Apple or email, add a player profile, complete a session, confirm history appears.

Partner mode (optional): iPad runs the display session; iPhone joins as coach via join code. Requires local network permission on both devices and an internet connection for relay coordination.

The app may transmit training session data using an anonymous identifier before account creation to support functionality. No personal account is required to begin training.
