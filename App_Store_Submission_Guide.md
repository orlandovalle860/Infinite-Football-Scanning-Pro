## Step 4: Build and Upload

### 4.1 Archive in Xcode
1. Open your project in Xcode
2. Select "Any iOS Device" as the target
3. Go to Product → Archive
4. Wait for the archive to complete

### 4.2 Upload to App Store Connect
1. In the Organizer window, select your archive
2. Click "Distribute App"
3. Select "App Store Connect"
4. Choose "Upload"
5. Follow the signing and distribution steps
6. Wait for processing (can take 10-30 minutes)

## Step 5: Submit for Review

### 5.1 Final App Store Connect Setup
1. Go back to App Store Connect
2. Select your app
3. Go to the "App Store" tab
4. Fill in all required information using the metadata from App_Store_Metadata.md

### 5.2 Submit for Review
1. Click "Submit for Review"
2. Answer any additional questions
3. Confirm submission

## Step 6: Review Process

### 6.1 What to Expect
- **Review Time**: 24-48 hours typically
- **Status Updates**: You'll receive emails about the review process
- **Possible Outcomes**:
  - **Approved**: App goes live on the App Store
  - **Rejected**: You'll receive feedback and can resubmit
  - **Metadata Rejected**: Only metadata needs changes

### 6.2 If Rejected
- Read the rejection feedback carefully
- Make necessary changes
- Resubmit with updated build or metadata
- Common issues:
  - Missing privacy policy
  - App crashes
  - Incomplete functionality
  - Metadata issues
