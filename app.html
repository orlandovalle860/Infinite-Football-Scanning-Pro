App Features

Everything you need for professional scanning training

Multiple Scanning Modes

Choose from various training modes including basic scanning, pattern recognition, and advanced awareness drills.

Voice Commands

Control the app hands-free with voice commands, keeping your focus on the game and your training.

Progress Tracking

Monitor your improvement with detailed analytics, performance graphs, and personalized insights.

Team Management

Create multiple player profiles and manage team training sessions with comprehensive reporting.

Customizable Settings

Adjust training parameters, audio feedback, and scanning zones to match your specific needs.

Offline & Online Training

Core solo training works without signing in. Some training data may be stored locally and may also be transmitted to support app functionality; when not signed in, data is associated with an anonymous identifier. Partner/coach features require a network connection.

System Requirements

Compatible with most modern devices

iOS Requirements

iOS 14.0 or later. Compatible with iPhone 8 and newer models. Requires 100MB of free storage space.

Android Requirements

Android 8.0 (API level 26) or later. Compatible with most modern Android devices. Requires 100MB of free storage space.

Internet Connection

Initial download requires internet connection. App works offline after installation. Optional cloud backup available.
