Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions

How does the app work?

The Infinite Football app uses your phone's camera to track your scanning patterns during training. It provides audio feedback and tracks your progress over time.

What devices are supported?

The app is available for iOS (iPhone 8 and newer) and Android (Android 8.0 and newer). It requires a camera and microphone for full functionality.

How much do training sessions cost?

Individual training sessions start at $150, group sessions at $75 per player, and team programs have custom pricing. Contact us for detailed quotes.

Is my data secure?

Yes, all data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share any personal information or training data. Your privacy is our priority.

Do you offer team training?

Yes, we offer comprehensive team training programs designed to improve collective awareness and coordination. Contact us for team-specific packages.

What's your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy on all merchandise. Return unused items in original packaging for a full refund or exchange.
