## Summary (for quick reference)

- You can train without creating a personal account.
- Some training data may be transmitted to our servers to support app functionality, even if you are not signed in. When not signed in, this data is associated with an anonymous identifier rather than a personal account.
- We may use anonymous identifiers to support functionality before account creation.
- If you sign in, account and training data may sync via secure cloud services (Supabase).
- We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

For full details, see `privacy_policy.html` or the hosted URL above.
